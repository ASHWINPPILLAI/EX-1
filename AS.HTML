Online Safety Tips for Teens

Why Online Safety Matters

As teens increasingly use the internet for learning, socializing, and entertainment, it is important to stay safe from online threats like cyberbullying, scams, and identity theft.

Top Safety Tips

Definition List

Phishing
Tricking users into giving away personal information via fake websites or emails.
Cyberbullying
Bullying that takes place over digital devices like phones or computers.

Ordered Nested List

  1. Protect Your Identity
    1. Never share passwords
    2. Use two-factor authentication
  2. Think Before You Share
    1. Don't post private photos
    2. Be cautious with location sharing

Unordered Nested List

  • Good Habits
    • Update your software
    • Use antivirus programs
  • Bad Practices to Avoid
    • Clicking on unknown links
    • Accepting friend requests from strangers
